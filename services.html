02. FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

We understand you may have questions about our health, safety, and environmental services. Here are answers to some common inquiries about how we can help protect your organization.

For specific questions about your unique requirements, please contact our team directly.

How often should safety training be conducted?

Safety training frequency depends on your industry, specific risks, and regulatory requirements. We recommend:

  • Annual refresher courses for most organizations
  • Quarterly training for high-risk environments
  • Immediate training for new hires or when introducing new equipment/processes

What's included in a risk assessment?

Our comprehensive risk assessments include:

  • On-site evaluation of all work areas and processes
  • Identification of potential hazards
  • Risk scoring based on likelihood and severity
  • Prioritized recommendations for risk mitigation
  • Compliance review with relevant regulations

Can you help with regulatory compliance?

Yes, we specialize in helping organizations navigate complex HSE regulations. Our services include:

  • Compliance audits and gap analysis
  • Documentation preparation and review
  • Staff training on regulatory requirements
  • Ongoing compliance monitoring

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Our emergency response team is available 24/7 and can typically:

  • Provide immediate phone consultation within minutes
  • Deploy on-site support within 2-4 hours for critical situations
  • Develop long-term solutions to prevent recurrence
